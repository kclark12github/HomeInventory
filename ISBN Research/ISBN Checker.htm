ISBN checker

  • Checks and corrects the check digit of ISBNs, ISSNs, ISMNs and 13-digit EANs, or if the check digit is not supplied, calculates the required check digit.
  • Hyphenates correctly most ISBNs and all ISMNs and ISSNs.
  • If the EAN (commercial barcode) is for a book, serial or item of music, extracts and displays the appropriate ISBN, ISSN or ISMN and calculates the correct check digit.
  • If the EAN is for a serial publication and is longer than 13 digits, also extracts the issue number information contained in the extra digits.

Help
The check digit is incorrect: If you are told the check digit is wrong, then this could mean that one or more of the other digits in the number is incorrect.

What format must I type the number in? You should be able to type it in exactly as it appears on the publication. e.g. for the ISBN 0-19-235423-X you could type all sorts of things including 019235423X, 019235423x, 0-19-235423-x and 0 1923 5423 X.

It says I have typed in too much, or too little, or letters instead of numbers. All characters will be ignored apart from 0-9, X, x, M and m. For an ISSN the number should be 7 or 8 digits long, for an ISBN or ISMN 9 or 10 digits, and for an EAN 12 or 13 digits (for a serial publication you can type in more, as the extra digits contain information about the issue number).
